FAQ

How do I use the freestyle word generator?

Select the language you want to practice with and select the speed(in seconds) you want the word to appear. You can select beats from the playlist below the word card. Also you can switch between light/dark mode and enlarge the word card.

Is there going to be support for additional languages?

Yes we are going to support more languages in the future. Let us know which language you want us to support we will work on it!

What features are coming up in the future?

We are going to add features such as:

  • Record your rap vocals with the beat
  • Record video with your rap recording
  • Freestyle rap leaderboards
