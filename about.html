About Freestyle Rap Coach

Freestyle Rap Coach is a website dedicated to providing tools & tips on improving your freestyle rap skills. Our main feature is the freestyle rap word generator which is inspired by RapScript. You can use it on a daily basis to hone your skills. We are planning to support more languages in the future. As for the articles page, you can find tips & tricks on how to improve your rap skills.
